blobopera.phoneme module¶

Phonemes used to represent lyrics for singing voice synthesis.

Note

This phoneme collection seems to have been designed with Italian or Eclesiastical Latin in mind, so it’s quite hard to describe certain sounds from other languages, like the German vowels ü, ö and ä.
